What is WallRank?

WallRank is a community-powered wallpaper discovery platform. We believe your desktop and mobile screen deserves the best — and the best is decided by the people, not an algorithm.

Every wallpaper on WallRank is voted on by real users. The more votes it gets, the higher it ranks. Simple, transparent, and community-first.
